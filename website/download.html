Installation Guide

Follow these steps to get Velvet up and running:

  1. Download the installer for your platform from the links above.
  2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  3. Add Velvet to your PATH environment variable for easy access.
  4. Verify the installation by running velvet --version in your terminal.
